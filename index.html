About James Dashner

James Dashner is the author of the #1 New York Times bestselling Mortality Doctrine trilogy, which includes The Eye of Minds, The Rule of Thoughts, and The Game of Lives, as well as the bestselling Maze Runner series, which includes The Maze Runner, The Scorch Trials, The Death Cure, The Kill Order, and The Fever Code.

About Mazerunner

When the doors of the lift crank open, the only thing Thomas can remember is his first name. He's not alone, though. Boys surrounding him welcome him to the Glade, a camp in the middle of a bizarre deadly maze.

Like Thomas, the Gladers don't know why or how they came to be there, or what's happening to the outside world. All they know is that they will risk everything to find out every morning when the walls emerge.

Press Release


The Maze Runner is a great book for anyone who is looking for a book like the hunger games for 3 main reasons. The first reason is you can enjoy this book even if you read a little everyday, for example if you read 2-3 chapters everyday. Another reason is that it is filled with emotions. For example one page there is a lot of fighting and the next page is filled with sadness after a character dies. The last reason is because this book is very different from the movie so even if you watched the movie you can still enjoy this book to its best content. These are the reasons why The Maze Runner is a great book for all ages.
